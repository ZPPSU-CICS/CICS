ABOUT CICS

History

The College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS) was established in 2005 with the vision of becoming a center of excellence in computing education in the region. Starting with just two programs and five faculty members, the college has grown exponentially over the years.

In 2010, CICS expanded its offerings to include specialized tracks in emerging technologies. By 2015, it had achieved accreditation for all its programs and established partnerships with leading tech companies.

Today, CICS stands as a premier institution for computing education, with state-of-the-art facilities, renowned faculty, and a track record of producing industry-ready graduates who excel in the global technology landscape.

Vision

A globally competitive center of excellence in computing education, research, and innovation that produces ethically-driven and socially responsible computing professionals.

Mission

To provide quality computing education through innovative instruction, relevant research, and responsive extension services that address the needs of the industry and community.
