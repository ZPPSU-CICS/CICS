About CICS

History of CICS Department

Ang CICS Department ay itinatag noong 2005 bilang bahagi ng university's commitment to IT education. Simula sa maliit na grupo ng 10 students, lumago ito sa isang full-fledged college na may mahigit 500 enrollees. Sa loob ng 18 taon, naging pioneer kami sa cybersecurity at AI programs, na may partnerships sa global tech firms tulad ng Cisco at Microsoft.

2005

Founded as Computer Science Dept.

2010

Renamed to CICS with expanded courses.

2020

Introduced futuristic IT labs with VR tech.

Ngayon, kami ay ang sentro ng innovation sa computing sciences.

Our Vision

To be the leading center of excellence in Information and Computing Sciences, producing globally competitive IT professionals equipped with cutting-edge skills and innovative mindset.
